Curious Minds Want to Know

How are pearls formed?

Pearls are formed inside the shells of certain mollusks as a defense mechanism against a potentially threatening irritant or parasite.

What makes pearls valuable?

The value of pearls is determined by their size, shape, color, luster, surface quality, and symmetry, with natural pearls being the most prized.

Are pearls sustainable?

Pearl farming can be sustainable, as it involves the cultivation of pearls in a way that minimizes environmental impact and supports the local ecosystem.

How do I care for pearls?

To maintain the luster of pearls, it's important to keep them away from harsh chemicals, perfumes, and cosmetics, and to gently wipe them with a soft cloth after wearing.

Can pearls be worn every day?

Pearls are durable and can be worn daily, but it's best to avoid exposing them to harsh conditions and to store them separately from other jewelry to prevent scratching.
