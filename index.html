FAQ

How are pearls farmed?

Pearls are farmed by carefully cultivating oysters in pristine waters and nurturing them to produce lustrous pearls. It's a delicate process that requires patience and expertise.

Are pearls sustainable?

Yes, pearl farming is an eco-friendly practice that promotes marine conservation and sustainable livelihoods for coastal communities.

What makes pearls valuable?

The rarity, size, shape, luster, and surface quality of pearls determine their value. Each pearl is a unique work of art, making it a prized possession.

Do pearls come in different colors?

Yes, pearls come in a variety of natural colors, including white, black, pink, and golden. Each color has its own unique charm and allure.

How do I care for pearls?

To keep your pearls looking their best, avoid contact with perfumes, lotions, and hairsprays. Clean them gently with a soft cloth and store them in a separate pouch to prevent scratching.
